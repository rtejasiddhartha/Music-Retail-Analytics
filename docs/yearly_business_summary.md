# Yearly Business Performance Summary

This document provides a consolidated, year-wise overview of business performance,
combining customer revenue, vendor procurement spend, profitability, and sales-channel
distribution. The analysis is derived from transactional order data and vendor invoices
and reflects **gross business performance** prior to fixed operational overheads.

---

---

## Year-wise Business Interpretation

### 2022 — Baseline Growth Year

- Vendor spend remained relatively low while customer revenue reached nearly **$500K**.
- Profit margin exceeded **150%**, indicating strong markup efficiency.
- Online revenue accounted for ~80% of total sales, establishing a digital-first model.
- Store pickup contributed meaningfully but remained secondary.

**Business Insight:**  
Efficient procurement combined with strong online demand drove high early profitability.

---

### 2023 — Revenue Stability with Margin Compression

- Total customer revenue remained stable compared to 2022.
- Vendor spend increased, leading to reduced profit margin (**84.86%**).
- Channel mix stayed consistent, showing stable customer behavior.
- Indicates rising procurement costs or tighter pricing strategies.

**Business Insight:**  
The business maintained revenue but faced cost-side pressure, reducing relative margins.

---

### 2024 — Revenue Expansion with Controlled Costs

- Highest customer revenue across all years (**$533K**).
- Vendor spend increased, but profit margin recovered to **~97%**.
- Store pickup revenue increased compared to previous years.
- Strong alignment between inventory procurement and customer demand.

**Business Insight:**  
2024 reflects operational maturity with balanced growth and cost control.

---

### 2025 — Partial-Year Snapshot with Elevated Margins

- Significantly lower total revenue due to **partial-year data**.
- Vendor spend is lower, but revenue-to-cost ratio appears unusually high.
- Profit margin exceeds **200%**, driven by timing effects:
  - Inventory purchased earlier
  - Fewer procurement invoices recorded
  - Continued order fulfillment from existing stock
- Online channel dominates even more strongly.

**Business Insight:**  
This year should be interpreted as an **incomplete accounting period**, not a decline or anomaly.

---

## Cross-Year Observations

- Online sales consistently contribute ~80% of revenue.
- Store pickup remains a stable secondary channel.
- Gross profitability remains positive across all years.
- Year-to-year fluctuations align with procurement timing, inventory carry-forward,
  and partial-period effects rather than demand collapse.

---

## Analytical Scope

This summary represents **gross-level financial performance** derived from transactional
data. It intentionally excludes fixed operational costs such as marketing, logistics,
platform fees, and staffing, making it suitable for:

- High-level business health checks
- Portfolio demonstrations
- Analytical storytelling
- Trend comparison across time

For deeper item-level profitability and loss attribution, refer to the
**profit & loss analysis** documentation.
